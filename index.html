Shape Shifter

Sculpture: protoboard, 14 pin header, brass rod
My second try soldering. I learned about the correct attachment points and how little solder is actually necessary. First, I soldered the pins, using a breadbord to stabilize the materials. Then I bent the brass rod and attached it to the protoboard in two places. The brass rod represents a path with a seemingly steady road that turns into a winding journey. It's also fun to spin the sculpture and look at it from different angles to discover different shapes.
